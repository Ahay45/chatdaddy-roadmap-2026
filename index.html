Stability & Foundation

May – Jul 2026

Fix what's broken, harden the core, and nail the first-run experience. No new growth until the foundation is solid. Every crash fixed is a retained user.

Infra & Product
Infra

Frontend V2 Launch

Ship the ground-up rebuild on Zustand + TanStack Router/Query/Virtual.

  • All 9 core modules migrated
  • Lazy loading + error boundaries on every route
  • Performance budget enforced (bundle ≤ 500 KB)
In Progress
Infra

Error Boundary Hardening

Eliminate the "Oops!" page for all 8 known crash-pattern categories (PR #3415 + follow-ups).

  • Replace 71 HIGH-severity render throws
  • Wrap 5 dynamic third-party imports
  • Automated audit CI gate → Lark alerts
In Progress
Product

Inbox 2.0

Redesigned inbox with unified chat view, smart filters, and quick replies.

  • Virtualized chat list (TanStack Virtual)
  • Pinned chats, labels, assignee filter
  • Bulk actions without race conditions
Planned
DX

UIUX Audit Automation

Daily scan of 16 anti-patterns → Lark report + auto-PR for fixable issues.

  • Baseline: 2,952 findings
  • Target: reduce by 40% in Q1
  • Lark dashboard with trend graph
In Progress
